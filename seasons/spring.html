Spring

Spring is a season know for it's warm weather snow storms. Some believe that the season is the result of nature being unable to decide between winter and summer and rapidly switching between the two instead.

It's known for new beginnings, new growth, new births. It's also known for being not quite warm enough to partake comfortably in many summertime activities, most notably water fights, swimming, and camping outside.

One of the most popular holidays in Spring is Easter. We celebrate Jesus' coming back to life after being dead for three days by hiding colored eggs and eating bean-shaped candy. Also there's something about a bunny.
