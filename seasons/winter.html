Winter

Winter is mostly known for being cold. Most people don't like being cold, so it's not a very popular season.

That being said, there are some activities that require cold, like skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, snowmobiling, ice fishing, sledding, snowman making, snow fort making, and swimming, but for crazy people. People who enjoy these activities tend to enjoy winter as well.

One of the most popular holidays in Winter is Christmas. We celebrate God becoming man by buying a bunch of stuff, flavoring everything like peppermint, and listening to frustratingly catchy music.
